# Privacy Policy for SpendWise

**Effective Date: December 29, 2025**

OptimisticByte ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SpendWise mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
- SMS Messages (for transaction parsing)
- Financial Transaction Details (Merchant name, amount, date)
- Category information

#### SMS Data (Sensitive Permission)
SpendWise requests permission to read and receive SMS messages. This data is used **exclusively** to:
- Automatically extract financial transaction information (e.g., bank alerts, UPI notifications).
- Facilitate the automated logging of expenses and income.

**Important:** Your SMS data is processed **locally on your device**. We do not upload, transmit, or share your raw SMS messages or their contents with any external servers.

## 2. Use of Data
SpendWise uses the collected data for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain the Service.
- To notify you about changes to our Service.
- To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service when you choose to do so.
- To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service.
- To monitor the usage of the Service.
- To detect, prevent and address technical issues.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
All transaction data and parsed information are stored in a **local database** on your device. 
- You are responsible for the security of your device.
- If you use the backup feature, the backup file is created on your device's local storage or your personal cloud storage (if configured), and remains under your control.

## 4. Disclosure of Data
We do **not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personal Data to outside parties. Your data stays on your device.

## 5. Third-Party Services
The Service does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
